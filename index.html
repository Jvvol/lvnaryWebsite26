The Band

About

LVNARY (looh-nuh-ree) is a progressive post-hardcore band born out of Morgantown, WV. Blending the intricate grooves of math rock, the emotional intensity of post-hardcore, and the fluid sophistication of swancore and fusion, LVNARY carves out a sonic identity that’s entirely their own.

Built for listeners who want music that challenges and grooves in equal measure.
